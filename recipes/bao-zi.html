包子 recipe

Finished product :)

Description

This is a simple 包子 recipe. It is made of a dough, a filling and a filling. The dough is made of flour, water and salt. The filling is made of a meat and a vegetable. The meat is ground and the vegetable is cut into small pieces.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg flour
  • 1 kg water
  • 1 kg salt
  • 1 kg ground meat
  • 1 kg vegetable

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, water and salt together.
  2. Add the ground meat and vegetable.
  3. Knead the dough.
  4. Cut the dough into small pieces.
  5. Cook the 包子 in a pan.
